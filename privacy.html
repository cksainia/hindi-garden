🗡️ Privacy Policy

Huntrix Aria · Hindi Slayer — Effective June 26, 2026
The short version: This is a free, offline-first learning app for one child. It shows no ads, has no accounts, and uses no third-party tracking or analytics. A child's photo never leaves the device. The only thing that can optionally leave the device is anonymous learning progress (stars and words practiced), and only if you turn on cross-device sync.

Who this is for

Hindi Slayer is a children's educational app intended to be set up and overseen by a parent or guardian. It is designed to be child-safe by default.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, address, phone number, or login.
  • No advertising, ad networks, or behavioral tracking. (An optional subscription unlocks Level 2 — see below — but we collect no personal data for it.)
  • No third-party analytics, trackers, cookies, or social-media SDKs.
  • No location data.
  • No audio recording. Speech is produced by your device's built-in text-to-speech; nothing is captured or sent anywhere.

The child's photo (avatar)

You may optionally add a photo as the child's character art. This image is stored only on the device, in local browser storage. It is never uploaded, never synced, and never transmitted off the device. Removing the app or clearing the app's data deletes it.

Optional cross-device sync

If you choose to create a "family code," the app can keep learning progress in sync between your own devices. When sync is on, the following is stored under a randomly generated, anonymous code on a hosting service (a Cloudflare Worker):

  • Learning progress only: stars earned, which words/lessons have been practiced, and review schedule.
  • No names, no photos, and no personal information are included.

The family code is the only key to that data. Anyone with the code can read the progress, so keep it private. You can turn sync off at any time from Settings → Device Sync → Disconnect, which stops any further syncing from that device.

Subscription (Hindi Slayer Plus)

Level 1 is free forever. An optional subscription, "Hindi Slayer Plus," unlocks Level 2 and future content. During the launch period it can be unlocked at no cost. When paid billing is enabled, payments are processed by the app store (Apple App Store / Google Play) or the payment provider — we never see or store card or payment details. The app stores only a simple "unlocked" flag (no personal information), which may sync between your own devices if you have turned on sync.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app collects no personal data and contains no ads or tracking, it is designed to align with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR provisions for children. A parent or guardian controls all setup, including the optional photo and sync.

How to delete data

Uninstalling the app, or clearing its data/site storage in your browser or device settings, permanently removes all local progress and the photo. To remove synced progress, disconnect sync; unused codes are not associated with any identity.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Effective" date above will be updated and the new version published at this page.
